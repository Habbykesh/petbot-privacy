🐾 PetBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information PetBot ("the Bot", "we", "our") collects when it is added to a Discord server, how that information is used, and how you can contact us with questions or requests about your data.

By using PetBot in a Discord server, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. What Information We Collect

To operate PetBot's virtual pet economy, tournament system, and leaderboards, we collect:

  • Discord User ID and Username — used to identify your pet, wallet, and progress within a server.
  • Server (Guild) ID — used to keep each server's economy and leaderboard separate from others.
  • Pet game data — your pet's name, type, level, experience, coin balance, feeding history, equipped and inventoried abilities, and tournament participation/results.
  • Message activity counts — when you send a message in a channel where coin-earning is enabled, we record that a message occurred so we can award in-game currency.
We do not store, log, or read the actual text/content of your messages. PetBot only detects that a message event happened in an eligible channel in order to award coins. The content of what you typed is never saved, processed, or reviewed by us or anyone else.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information above is used exclusively to operate PetBot's game features, including:

  • Tracking your pet's stats, abilities, and progress
  • Calculating and awarding coins from chat activity and daily claims
  • Running tournaments, calculating battle outcomes, and distributing prize payouts
  • Displaying leaderboards and rankings within your server
  • Assigning and managing Discord roles tied to your pet's type, evolution stage, or tournament achievements

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Bot's game functionality.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

PetBot stores data using the following infrastructure:

  • MongoDB Atlas — stores persistent game data such as pet profiles, tournament records, and server configuration.
  • Redis — temporarily buffers message-activity counts before they are saved to the main database, to keep the Bot fast and responsive at scale.

Both services are managed through Railway, our hosting provider. No data is sold or shared with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • If you disown your pet using the Bot's commands, that pet's data is permanently deleted.
  • If a pet dies due to inactivity (per the Bot's in-game feeding rules), its data is retained only as a historical record unless you contact us to request deletion.
  • If the Bot is removed from a server, that server's configuration and associated game data may be deleted during routine cleanup.
  • You may request full deletion of your data at any time by contacting us using the details in Section 7.

5. Children's Privacy

PetBot is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Discord's own Terms of Service require users to meet a minimum age requirement to use Discord at all. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data through the Bot, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as PetBot's features change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
